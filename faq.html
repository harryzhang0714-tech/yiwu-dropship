Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dropshipping services

We typically provide sourcing results within 24-48 hours. This includes supplier verification, wholesale pricing, and product availability checks. For specialized or hard-to-find products, it may take slightly longer.
MOQ varies by product. Many suppliers offer low MOQs of 1-10 units for dropshipping. We'll negotiate the best terms on your behalf and provide options for different budget levels.
Every order goes through our QC process. We check product condition, packaging integrity, and accuracy. We provide photo and video reports upon request. Defective items can be returned or disposed of according to your preferences.
Shipping times vary by method: Express (5-10 days), Standard (10-20 days), and Economy (20-40 days). We offer multiple carriers including YunExpress, 4PX, CNE, USPS, and Yanwen to suit your needs.
Yes! We offer custom brand boxes, logo printing, thank you cards, and retail-ready packaging. Custom packaging typically adds 2-3 days to processing time and costs vary based on your specifications.
We accept PayPal, Wise, bank transfers, and Alipay/WeChat Pay for Chinese suppliers. You pay for products + shipping + service fee after confirming the quote. No upfront costs for sourcing.
We handle returns professionally. You can choose to have defective items returned to your local address, disposed of in China, or credited back. We provide documentation and handle dispute resolution.
Our service fee depends on order volume and service level. For high-volume partners, we offer competitive rates. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your business needs.
