# How to Start Dropshipping from Yiwu: Complete Beginner's Guide

Yiwu is known as "the world's supermarket" and is the largest small commodity wholesale market in China. For entrepreneurs looking to start dropshipping business, Yiwu is an ideal sourcing hub.

## Why Yiwu?

- **Massive Product Variety**: Over 1.8 million products across 64 categories
- **Competitive Pricing**: Factory-direct prices with no minimum order requirements
- **Easy Shipping**: Well-developed logistics network to worldwide destinations
- **Quality Control**: You can personally inspect products before shipping

## How to Start

### Step 1: Find Reliable Suppliers
Connect with verified Yiwu suppliers through:
- Trade shows
- Online marketplaces (Alibaba, 1688)
- Local sourcing agents

### Step 2: Sample Products
Always order samples before listing to verify quality.

### Step 3: Set Up Your Store
Choose your platform:
- Shopify
- WooCommerce
- TikTok Shop

### Step 4: Test with Small Orders
Start with small test orders to verify supplier reliability and shipping times.

## Conclusion

Yiwu offers incredible opportunities for dropshippers. Start small, test thoroughly, and scale gradually.

*Happy dropshipping!*
